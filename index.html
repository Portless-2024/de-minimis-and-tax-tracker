This page is for reference only and is not legal or tax advice. Confirm the current rule with the destination authority before you rely on it.

How to read this
De minimis threshold: the order value below which duties are reduced or waived. Some countries set a separate, lower cutoff for tax than for duty.
Import tax: whether VAT, GST, or sales tax is owed, and who collects it. In many markets the seller or marketplace now collects at checkout.
Status: whether the rule is unchanged, under review, scheduled to change, or already gone.
